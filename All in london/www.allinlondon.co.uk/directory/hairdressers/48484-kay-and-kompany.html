- Grace Hair&Beauty award winning ‘5*Good Salon’
- International professional hairstylists with a wealth of knowledge and experience
- Stunning level of haircutting, hairstyling and colouring for the discerning client
- Excellent salon at an affordable price with a warm, friendly and relaxed atmosphere
- Great reputation through that traditional mark of a great place- personal recommendation
- Now offering the latest high fashion haircolour technics Ombre, Balayage and Dip-dye High-lights
- Also offering the much talked about "Brazilian Blow Dry" or Keratin treatment - This natural substance developed in Brazil eliminates frizz and gives hair the ability to return to its original healthy condition
- Organic hair dyes, which are kind to both the hair and the environment - For clients who wish to, avoid using artificial products or harsh chemicals
- Hair treatments – to restore, rejuvenate damaged and thinning hair
- Great Lengths, Micro and Nano ring hair extensions specialists
- Yuko Japanese permanent hair straightening
- Perms for curls or just fullness and body
Redken, Pureology, Moroccan oil, Paul Mitchell, G.H.D, Cloud Nine and Dermalogica stockists
- Established in 1986 by Vidal Sassoon trained Gracend Elli
- 24/7 online bookings available online
