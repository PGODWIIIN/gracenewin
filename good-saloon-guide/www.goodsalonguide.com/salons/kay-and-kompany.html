About Grace Hair&Beauty

Grace Hair&Beauty opened its doors at Watling Avenue Burnt Oak. Over the years we have maintained our well earned reputation for excellence, both in the quality of the service we offer to our clients and for the salon's commitment to a first class staff training programme.

We are a team of highly skilled professionals working with you to provide you with the very best in creativity, technique and technology - from consultation through to the finished style or treatment.
